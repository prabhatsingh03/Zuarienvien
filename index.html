Our first project — a 180 KLPD grain-based ethanol distillery in Aira, Lakhimpur Kheri, Uttar Pradesh — is a key step in our journey to scale up to 1000 KLPD across India.

The Synergy Behind Our Sustainability

Our Story

Founded as a strategic joint venture between Zuari Industries Ltd. and Envien International, ZEBPL reflects a shared vision to advance India’s renewable energy goals. With its first 180 KLPD distillery set on 20.05 acres of vested land in Aira, the venture is set to scale up capacity to 1000 KLPD across the country.

Joint Venture Advantage

ZEBPL exemplifies Indo-European cooperation, combining Zuari's financial strength and local excellence with Envien Group's global technology and operational expertise to deliver high-quality, scalable bioenergy solutions for India.

Fueling Progress, One Drop at a Time

Overview of Operations

Our flagship project is a 180 KLPD grain-based ethanol distillery in Aira, designed to process rice or maize sourced from Lakhimpur Kheri and nearby districts. The plant integrates advanced ZLD (Zero Liquid Discharge) systems to ensure environmental compliance and efficiency.

Technology & Innovation

The distillery incorporates advanced automation and eco-friendly practices, with global technology inputs from Envien Group to ensure maximum productivity and minimum environmental footprint.

Pure Energy. Powerful Impact.

Fuel-Grade Ethanol

BIS-compliant fuel-grade ethanol used by leading Oil Marketing Companies (OMCs) for blending with petrol to reduce fossil fuel use and enhance energy security.

DDGS

Dried Distillers Grains with Solubles (DDGS) serve as a nutrient-rich and highly palatable animal feed, primarily for livestock.

Fusel Oil

A valuable byproduct used in various industrial applications, including the manufacturing of solvents and chemicals.
